INTRODUCING: FRESH AND WARM TAKEAWAY CAKE

  • By MRS STARBUCKS
  • OCTOBER, 22 2020
  • 0 comments

We have introduced new and improved seating inside. Because of COVID-19 we had to change our seating and space in the café, we made sure every table and seat has been placed 2 meters apart which allows every customer to be safe during this pandemic, we have also added new chairs in the café, instead of the old boring chairs we added new heated and vibrating chairs which allow customers to be more comfortable and to have a great experience all together. After our customers leave their tables, we make sure everything is disinfected to continue making sure our customers are safe. For more and future news, check out our blog page.
