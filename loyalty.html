Loyalty Scheme

What is the loyalty scheme?

Our Loyalty scheme is very simple but very rewarding, it enables customers to earn stamps through transactions, which rewards our beloved customers.



How does it work?

Our loyalty scheme uses cards and stamps. The first time you visit our caf√© we will furnish you with a loyalty scheme card. There will be 10 slots on the card and every time you purchase something from the menu, we will provide you a stamp on the card after every purchase. After 5 stamps you get 50% off on your other and on the 10th stamp you get a free drink of your choice, any size and even includes any limited-edition drinks. After the 10th stamp and after you have used your reward, we take away your old card and provide you with a new one that way our customers can continue to be rewarded with discounts or free drinks. This is a lovely way to give back to our beloved customers. We are working on adding this rewards system to an application form that our beloved customers can download to their phones, this way we help out the environment by not using cards and space on our customers wallets or wherever they keep our cards.
